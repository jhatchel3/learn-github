About Me

My journey to software development is different than most. Growing up I never had an interest in computers. I always leaned towards more physical exploits; mainly bodybuidling. From the age of thirteen until the age thirty-two my obession was bodybuilding. With that obession I decided to get a bachelors degree in Kinesiology. Upon graduation I landed my dream job; selling personal training at LA Fitness in Atlanta, Georgia. After a couple years I became bored with fitness because I felt like I had basically learned everything, and all the new information was basically just a deviation from what already was an optimal way to achieve any fitness goal.

One day I had a great idea for a web site that had a catalog of fitness videos that people could subscribe to. I searched far and wide for a developer that would take on the project and everyone I spoke with had no idea how to develop it. Now I know that the people I was contacting where merely front end developers which is not a bad thing but that is just to say that they had little to no experience in developing full stack applications. After about 6 months of trying to find someone else to develop the project, I decided I would learn how to do it myself. It has been a long road and I am excited about the ability to help millions of peole with software that I have developed.

A wise man once said "If you want to fast, go alone; if you want to go far go together". What makes software development so amazing to me is that you will never know everything. Working with a team of fellow engineers is my ultimate goal. I want to go to work everyday and work on challenging portions of a project with a team of individuals. Working with a team will allow us all to learn from each other, see other points of view and grow as a company and individuals. I have spent the last three years of my professional career as the manager of a restaurant. During this time I have truly realized the value of a cohesive team. I know a team is only as strong as its weakest link but also with the right mindset can accomplish anything.

My services include front end web development using HTML5/CSS3. I also can provide full stack software development using Javascript and Node. I also can offer expertise in libraries such as Node Express and Boostrap. With my extensive study of relational databases I will be able to use your market research and turn that data into a viable software solution for your customers.
